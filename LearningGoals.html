By the end of the course I would like to be able to code fluently in a wide range of programing languages and feel confident with my understanding of all topic and subject areas that we cover in the course. I would also like to do something worthwhile with my spare time like set up a buisness.

My ideal job would be a job in web development or internet security. if I do not acheive this I would like to work for myself in my own company and set up a small buisness or work freelance in web and application devlopment.
